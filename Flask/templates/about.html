The goal of this application is to see what heroes and maps you play best on!

As someone who plays a lot of competetive Overwatch, I've always been interested in maximizing my performance. I have tried climbing up the ranks through multiple means (LFG, one tricking, filling, etc.). I've also used third party sites like Overbuff to extract as much information as I can about my performances.

With my new found passion for data analytics, I decided to take matters into my own hands and create something to help myself and others who are in a slump.
